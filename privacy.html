Privacy

No lawyers. Just facts.

Video

Video is peer-to-peer via WebRTC. It goes directly from your device to the other person. Our server never sees it, touches it, or stores it.

Server

Our backend (Socket.io on Render) only sees your socket ID and the fact that you want to be matched. It stores nothing to disk.

Cookies / Tracking

None. We don't use analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies. Zero.

Age

You must be 18+. We can't technically enforce this, so we're asking nicely. Don't be weird.
